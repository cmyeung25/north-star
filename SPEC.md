# Product Spec (Phase 1–3)

## Phase 1 — Core Planning Foundation

- Scenario creation with basic life-stage framing.
- Event timeline inputs (income, expenses, major life events).
- Assumptions management (inflation, growth, taxes).
- Offline-first PWA shell and baseline navigation.

## Phase 2 — Simulation & Insights

- Run multi-year cashflow simulations in the browser.
- Stress testing with scenario comparisons.
- Alerts for plan gaps and risk thresholds.
- Exportable reports for personal use.

## Phase 3 — Collaboration & Continuity

- Multi-device sync via a thin backend.
- Secure sharing with advisors or family members.
- Version history and scenario branching.
- Enhanced accessibility and localization.

## Scope boundaries

- **No investment advice. Planning & simulation only.**
- No product recommendations or sales.
- No financial calculations beyond planning simulations.
